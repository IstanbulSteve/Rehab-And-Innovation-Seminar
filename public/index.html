About the Seminar

The programme is built from submitted abstracts and presentations by clinicians, researchers, and individuals with lived experience of neurological injury. The seminar provides a shared space to examine innovative, practical approaches to movement rehabilitation through discussion, case material, and demonstration.

Call for Abstracts

Please state whether you would like your abstract considered for oral or e-poster presentation. Abstracts should be a maximum of 800 words and contain a Title and a section each for Aim, Methods and Results & Observations, with a maximum of 1 diagram and 1 table per section.
